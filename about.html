About Us

Compassionate care with a family-first approach

Welcome to Lakshmi Vijaya Medicare Hospital, Tagarapuvalasa's trusted healthcare center for comprehensive, compassionate, and advanced medical care.

Established in 2022, we are committed to offering world-class treatment with a focus on safety, comfort, and excellence. Our mission is to make quality healthcare affordable and accessible to all.

Located in Tagarapuvalasa, Visakhapatnam, our 102-bed modern facility offers a wide range of services in obstetrics, gynecology, pediatrics, neonatology, general medicine, laparoscopy, and surgery.

At Lakshmi Vijaya, we combine personalized attention, advanced technology, and a holistic approach to ensure the best outcomes for every patient.

We are committed to patient-centered care, empowering families, and delivering safe, ethical, and affordable healthcare. Our skilled team and the latest medical technology ensure exceptional care for all our patients.

Our vision is to be the leading healthcare provider in the region, known for our commitment to excellence, compassion, and innovation in medical care.
