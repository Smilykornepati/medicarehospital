About Us

Welcome to Lakshmi Vijaya Medicare Hospital, Tagarapuvalasa's trusted healthcare center for comprehensive, compassionate, and advanced medical care.

Established in 2022, we are committed to offering world-class treatment with a focus on safety, comfort, and excellence. Our mission is to make quality healthcare affordable and accessible to all.

Located in Tagarapuvalasa, Visakhapatnam, our 102-bed modern facility offers a wide range of services in obstetrics, gynecology, pediatrics, neonatology, general medicine, laparoscopy, and surgery, backed by a skilled team and the latest medical technology.

At Lakshmi Vijaya, we combine personalized attention, advanced technology, and a holistic approach to ensure the best outcomes for every patient. We are committed to patient-centered care, empowering families, and delivering safe, ethical, and affordable healthcare.
